How to Play

Welcome to the Bipartite Matching Game! Your goal is to find the maximum-weight matching in a bipartite graph.

Rules:

  • Click on edges to highlight them and add them to your matching
  • Double-click on edge weights to edit them (values between -1 and 1)
  • Each node can only be used once in a matching
  • Try to maximize the total weight of your matching

Controls:

  • Single click/tap: Toggle edge highlight
  • Double click/tap: Edit edge weight
  • Drag nodes: Rearrange the graph layout
  • Reset button: Generate a new random graph
  • Check button: Verify if your matching is optimal

Scoring:

  • Each edge has a weight between -1 and 1
  • Your score is the sum of weights of highlighted edges
  • Try to find the matching with the highest possible score!
